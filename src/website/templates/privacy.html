Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

Templar is an AI-powered template generation system. When you purchase a template through our marketplace integrations (Gumroad, Payhip, LemonSqueezy, Etsy), those platforms share your order information with us for delivery purposes only.

If you connect your Notion workspace for direct template delivery, we store:

  • Your Notion workspace ID and name
  • OAuth access and refresh tokens (encrypted at rest)
  • The specific page URL we create for your template delivery

We never access, read, or modify any other pages in your Notion workspace.

What we don't collect

We don't collect names, email addresses, payment information, or any other personal data beyond what's needed for template delivery. Payment processing is handled entirely by Gumroad, Payhip, LemonSqueezy, and Etsy.

Cookies

We don't use cookies on the Templar website. No tracking, no analytics, no third-party scripts.

Data retention

Notion OAuth tokens are deleted when you request account disconnection or when you request deletion of your data. Order data is retained for 2 years for accounting purposes.

Your rights

You can request deletion of all your data at any time by emailing support@templar.so. We will delete all stored Notion tokens and buyer records within 30 days.

Third parties

We use Anthropic and OpenAI for AI template generation. Those services process your template descriptions under their own privacy policies. We don't share your Notion workspace data with them.

Changes

If this privacy policy changes, we'll update the date at the top. We don't make retroactive changes that expand data collection.

Contact

Questions? Email support@templar.so
